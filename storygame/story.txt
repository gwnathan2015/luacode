now, in 256 CE of the planet everliynn, you alone can plan and manage to defeat the dwarven king, starting from the last free, big city, fort city, you must free the world of the dwarven empire, and you are not alone in your fights, you have the wizard, who will gladly sell you your potions in return for coins gained by defeating dwarves, the farmer, who will gladly sell you food to heal your health after a great fight with dwarves, and your most loyal ally, the swordsman, who pledged to protect and help you in combat
